# 🚀 PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)

## Agency Website (Conversion-Focused, Animation-Driven)

---

## 1. PRODUCT OVERVIEW

### 🎯 Goal

Build a **high-converting agency website** that:

* Showcases design & development capability
* Creates a strong first impression
* Converts visitors into **project inquiries**

---

## 2. CORE OBJECTIVE

> Turn visitors into clients through clear messaging, strong visuals, and a frictionless contact flow.

---

## 3. TARGET USERS

### 👤 Primary Users

* Business owners
* Startup founders
* Creators needing websites

### 🧠 User Intent

* “I need a website”
* “I want something better than basic templates”
* “I want a professional team”

---

## 4. WEBSITE STRUCTURE (PAGES & SECTIONS)

> Single-page scroll website

---

### 🧩 PAGE: Home (`/`)

---

## 🔥 SECTION 1: HERO

### Purpose:

* Grab attention instantly
* Communicate value

### Content:

* Headline
* Subtext
* Primary CTA

### CTA:

* **Primary:** Start a Project
* **Secondary (optional):** View Our Work

---

## ✍️ SECTION 2: SCROLL TEXT (PINNED)

### Purpose:

* Emotional storytelling
* Differentiate from competitors

### Content:

> Build something that means something. Not just something that works.

### Behavior:

* Word-by-word reveal on scroll
* Section pinned during animation

### CTA:

* None (focus = message)

---

## 🎯 SECTION 3: SERVICES (MARQUEE)

### Purpose:

* Show capabilities quickly

### Content:

* Web Design
* Web Development
* UI/UX Systems
* Performance Optimization
* Branding (optional)

### CTA:

* **Explore Services**

---

## 🎥 SECTION 4: WORK (HORIZONTAL SCROLL)

### Purpose:

* Build trust through past work

### Content:

* Featured projects (3–5)
* Title + short description

### CTA:

* **View Project**
* **See More Work**

---

## 📞 SECTION 5: CONTACT / CTA

### Purpose:

* Convert user into lead

### Content:

* Headline
* Short supporting text
* Form

### Headline:

> Let’s build something that stands out.

### CTA:

* **Start Your Project**
* **Send Inquiry**

---

## 5. CONTACT FORM REQUIREMENTS

### Fields:

* Name (required)
* Email (required)
* Project Type (dropdown)
* Budget (dropdown)
* Description (textarea)

### Behavior:

* Validation required
* Success message after submission

---

## 6. FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS

### Must Have:

* Smooth scrolling
* Responsive design
* Fast loading
* Working contact form (connected to backend)

### Should Have:

* Scroll-triggered animations
* Hover interactions (red accent system)

---

## 7. DESIGN REQUIREMENTS

### 🎨 Color System

* Black (primary)
* White (content)
* Red (hover & interaction only)

### Rules:

* Default UI = black & white
* Red appears only on hover / interaction

---

### 🔤 Typography

* Bold headings
* Clean sans-serif font
* Large scale for hero & sections

---

## 8. ANIMATION REQUIREMENTS

### Tools:

* GSAP + ScrollTrigger
* Lenis (smooth scroll)

### Key Animations:

* Hero fade-in
* Text reveal (pinned section)
* Marquee scroll
* Horizontal work scroll

---

## 9. USER FLOW

### Primary Flow:

Landing → Scroll → Understand → Click CTA → Fill Form → Submit

---

## 10. FILE STRUCTURE (IMPORTANT)

> All file and folder names must be **clear, readable, and descriptive**

### ✅ Rules:

* Use full words (no abbreviations)
* Use meaningful names
* Keep structure organized

---

### 📁 Example Structure:

```
/app
  /components
    HeroSection.jsx
    ScrollTextSection.jsx
    ServicesMarquee.jsx
    WorkHorizontalScroll.jsx
    ContactSection.jsx

  /styles
    globalStyles.css

  page.jsx
```

---

### ❌ Avoid:

* `comp1.jsx`
* `sectionA.jsx`
* `temp.js`

---

### ✅ Use:

* `HeroSection.jsx`
* `ContactForm.jsx`
* `ProjectShowcase.jsx`
